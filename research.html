As part of the 2018 CRA-W DREU program, Olivia Richards was matched with Julie Kientz at the University of Washington, in the department of Human Centered Design and Engineering. Dr. Kientz studies Human-Computer Interaction, Ubiquitous Computing, and Health Informatics and is passionate about the intersection of those fields with healthy living and learning. Olivia is a rising senior Mathematics student at the Pennsylvania State University, in University Park, Pennsylvania. She plans to complete her Bachelor's degree in Mathematics in May 2019 to then obtain her Ph.D. in Human-Centered Computing from another research institution.
